The starry sky is of amazing beauty

The starry night stretches endlessly above, its quiet glow reminding us how small we are and how infinite the universe’s mysteries truly remain

Interesting facts

Millions of stars are invisible by day, hidden by our sun’s bright radiance

The Milky Way galaxy holds over 100 billion stars in a spiral disk of gas

Stargazing is best far from city lights — light pollution hides faint stars

Auroras occur when solar particles excite Earth’s atmosphere near poles

When we look up at the sky, the stars seem to twinkle just for us. In reality, it is the light that has traveled for thousands of years to reach your eyes right now. Think about it: some of the stars you see have long since faded, but their light still continues its journey through the Universe.
